Selena Gomez

Selena Gomez is an American singer, songwriter, actress, and producer. Selena Gomez was known for her starring role on the Disney show Wizards of Waverly Place and her pop hit songs such as “Same Old Love” and “Come and Get It.”

Cardi B

Cardi B is an American singer, songwriter, and actress. Cardi B rose to fame with her song "Bodak Yellow" in 2017.

Megan Thee Stallion

Megan Thee Stallion is an American rapper, singer, and songwriter. Megan Thee Stallion is known for her confidence, sensuality, and explicit lyrics.

Roddy Ricch

Roddy Ricch is an American rapper, singer, and songwriter. Roddy Ricch released his debut album, Please Excuse Me for Being Antisocial, which scored many top hit singles and was nominated for 3 Grammys.

Blueface

Blueface is an American rapper and songwriter. Blueface is best known for the song “Thotiana,” which peaked at No. 8 on the Billboard Hot 100 and remixed by many other celebrities, giving him more exposure.

DaBaby

DaBaby is an American rapper, singer, and songwriter. DaBaby won fans over with his debut album Baby on Baby, being on top of the Billboard charts.

Harry Styles

Harry Styles is an English singer, songwriter, and actor. His musical career began as a solo competitor on the British music contest on The X Factor. Harry Styles went from being in a boy band called one direction to be back to solo with even more fame.

Billie Eillish

Billie Eilish is an American singer and songwriter. Billie Eilish went viral at the age of 14 years old when she uploaded "Ocean Eyes" for her dance teacher to choreograph. She soon quickly rose to fame and has of now 6 Grammys.

The Weeknd

The Weeknd is a Canadian singer and songwriter. The Weeknd has one 3 grammies in an urban contemporary album for "Starboy" in 2018 and for "Beauty Behind the Madness" in 2016. He won the best Rhythm and Blues performance for "Earned It" in 2016.

Khalid

Khalid is an American singer and songwriter. He is best know for the debut single "Location"; which hit number 20 on the Billboard's Mainstream Rhythm and Blues/Hip Hop Airplay chart in 2016.
